Credits & Licenses

The artists and creators whose work helps give Vindicator its voice and silhouette.

The galaxy is assembled from original Vindicator work and carefully selected assets whose creators chose to share them. These are the credits that travel with every build. CC0 sources are listed for provenance even though the license asks nothing in return; the CC-BY and MIT sources must be named wherever the build goes.

Music

  • “Miami Vice” — Music by Karl Casey @ White Bat Audio. The gameplay bed. Royalty-free / copyright-safe, free for commercial use with credit. Source: https://whitebataudio.com
  • “SPACE 3 title song” — tracker module from The Mod Archive (#69599, .s3m format). Converted to Ogg Vorbis for engine playback. Source: https://modarchive.org/
  • “space 12” — Pierrick. Tracker module from The Mod Archive (#147849, .xm format). Converted to Ogg Vorbis for engine playback. Source: https://modarchive.org/

Ship & station art

  • Foozle “Void” packs — foozlecc.itch.io. The player’s hull, the three faction fleet hulls (Kla’ed, Nairan, Nautolan), and the station hulls (which reuse the fleet sprites). CC0. Source: https://foozlecc.itch.io/
  • Tiny Spaceships by DisruptorArt. The independent Prospector and Salvager Tug hulls use frames derived from this pack, licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 4.0. Source: https://disruptorart.itch.io/tiny-ships-free-spaceships

The wreckage hulks in the salvage graveyard are Vindicator-original (AI-assisted) sprites, not third-party art.

  • Space Shooter - Ship Constructor by anim86. Selected gun mounts, cannon rounds, missiles, and torpedoes. Credited use from the author’s game-asset constructor pack. Source: https://anim86.itch.io/space-shooter-ship-constructor

Shaders

  • PixelPlanets by Deep-Fold. The asteroid shader (the planets, stars, and black holes are Vindicator originals — see Celestial Bodies). MIT. Source: https://github.com/Deep-Fold/PixelPlanets
  • PixelSpace by Deep-Fold. The mid-layer starfield shader behind every system sky. MIT. Source: https://github.com/Deep-Fold/PixelSpace
  • Deep Space Shader by Xeraesh. The nebula haze layer behind every system sky. MIT. Source: https://godotshaders.com/shader/deep-space-shader/

Module icons

  • game-icons.net — icons by Lorc, Delapouite, and sbed. The module glyph icons (weapons, engines, tweaks, drones) shown on grid inventory cells, the arena palette, and tooltip cards. Licensed under Creative Commons Attribution 3.0. Source: https://game-icons.net

Sound & UI

  • “Strange Sweeping Synth Horn 3” by SomeUsername0. Edited into the gateway-fling release cue. Creative Commons Zero 1.0 (CC0). Source: https://freesound.org/people/SomeUsername0/sounds/846357/
  • Kenney — kenney.nl. Sci-fi sound effects (explosions, dock clunk, warp whoosh, engine loop, UI click, energy blasts, energy impacts) and the Pixel UI / Input Prompts texture sets used across the HUD and menus. CC0. Source: https://kenney.nl
  • rubberduck — the base cutting/mechanical textures behind the mining and salvage tool loops (30 CC0 SFX Loops, OpenGameArt). CC0. Source: https://opengameart.org/content/30-cc0-sfx-loops

Fonts

All under the SIL Open Font License.

  • Geist by Vercel — the body font. Source: https://vercel.com/font
  • Asimovian by Carolina Short — the display/title font. Source: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Asimovian
  • JetBrains Mono by JetBrains — the monospace readout font. Source: https://www.jetbrains.com/lp/mono/

Engine

  • Godot Engine — the engine this runs in, and the runtime for every shader and scene above. MIT. Source: https://godotengine.org

Fonts

  • Doto by Óliver Lalan and the Doto Project Authors. Combat damage numbers use this typeface under the SIL Open Font License 1.1. Source: https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Doto

CC0 assets do not require attribution, but their provenance and the complete MIT/OFL/Creative Commons notices remain beside the source assets in the project distribution.
