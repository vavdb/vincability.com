System spine

Hull = faction body; Hardpoints = neutral sockets; Weapon = modular item (template plus optional module wrapper); FX/art = item family and recolorable; Default loadout is not a unique inseparable gun.

That sentence is the production contract. A faction owns the readable body and markings of its craft. A weapon owns its reusable mount and shot language. Content chooses which weapons a hull starts with, while salvage, fabrication, shops, and loadout tools may move the same item family onto another hull.
